1. Important Notice Regarding Age and Children's Privacy

Bloxfit does not restrict access based on age, and the App may be used by children. However, we take children's privacy seriously:

  • During onboarding, our advertising SDK (Yodo1 Mas, described below) collects the user's age in order to determine whether the user is under 13 years old.
  • If a user indicates they are under 13 years old, the App automatically limits advertising to non-personalized ads only, in compliance with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act ("COPPA") and Google Play's Families/child-directed advertising policies.
  • We do not knowingly collect more personal information from children under 13 than is reasonably necessary for this age-gating and ad-configuration purpose.
  • Parents and guardians: if you believe your child has provided us with personal information beyond what is described above, please contact us at zega.apps.dev@gmail.com so we can address it.

3. AI Outfit Detection Feature

When you upload a photo or screenshot of a Roblox outfit:

  1. The image is sent to Google's Gemini API for processing, in order to detect and identify the outfit items shown.
  2. The identified items are then matched against the Roblox API to retrieve public information such as item name, price, creator, thumbnail image, and a direct link to the Roblox catalog/store page.
  3. We do not store the uploaded images on our servers, and we do not retain a history of the outfits you have analyzed. Images are processed transiently for the sole purpose of generating the analysis result and are not retained by us afterward.
  4. Please note that your image is still transmitted to Google's Gemini API for processing; Google's own privacy practices govern how Gemini handles data submitted to it. We encourage you to review Google's Privacy Policy.
  5. Free users receive 3 free AI outfit analyses. After that, watching a rewarded ad (via Yodo1 Mas) is required to unlock each additional analysis, unless you have purchased the ad-removal membership described in Section 6.

Bloxfit is an independent application and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Roblox Corporation or Google/Gemini. All Roblox trademarks, item data, and imagery obtained through the Roblox API remain the property of Roblox Corporation and/or the relevant content creators.
